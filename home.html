About me

I am a soon to be middle-aged man from Sweden with a wide variety of interests. Hiking, hunting, programming, photography, studying whatever takes my interest (mostly technical stuff).

At the age of 19 I moved to Japan and stayed there for two years studying Japanese at a language school in Kyoto. I can still speak and (barely) read Japanese and the country has always had a special place in my heart. Thus I will also write about Japan from time to time.

I work for a Japanese robot manufacturer as a software engineer and also do some freelance programming and consulting. While originally trained as a mechanical engineer I have always loved computers and digital technology. Even though I only had a little experience in Python at the time, my first job involved developing software and I never went back after that. I have also worked, and still do from time to time, with automation and system integration.
